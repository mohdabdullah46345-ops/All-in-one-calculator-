About FinCalc Pro

FinCalc Pro is a free, easy-to-use financial toolkit that brings four popular calculators together in one place. Whether you are planning a monthly investment, taking a home loan, opening a fixed deposit, or working out tax on an invoice, you can get instant, accurate estimates without any sign-up.

📈 SIP Calculator

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) lets you invest a fixed amount in mutual funds every month. Our SIP calculator uses standard compound-growth formulas to show your total invested amount, estimated returns, and the projected maturity value.

🏦 EMI Calculator

Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) is the fixed payment you make towards a loan each month. Enter the loan amount, interest rate, and tenure to see your monthly EMI along with the total interest and total payment.

💰 FD Calculator

A Fixed Deposit (FD) grows your savings at a fixed rate. This calculator uses quarterly compounding to estimate your maturity value and the interest earned over the chosen period.

🧾 GST Calculator

The GST calculator helps you add GST to a base amount or remove GST from an inclusive amount, and also splits the tax into CGST and SGST components.
