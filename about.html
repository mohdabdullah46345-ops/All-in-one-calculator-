About FinCalc Pro

Last updated: June 2026

FinCalc Pro is a free online financial calculator created to make everyday money calculations simple, fast, and accessible to everyone. Instead of juggling multiple apps or complex spreadsheets, you get four essential calculators in a single, clean interface that works on both desktop and mobile.

What we offer

  • SIP Calculator – Project the future value of your monthly mutual fund investments.
  • EMI Calculator – Work out your monthly loan installment, total interest, and total payment.
  • FD Calculator – Estimate fixed deposit maturity value using quarterly compounding.
  • GST Calculator – Add or remove GST and view the CGST/SGST split instantly.

Our mission

Financial planning should not require expensive software or specialized knowledge. Our goal is to give students, salaried professionals, small business owners, and first-time investors a reliable set of tools to understand the numbers behind their decisions — completely free of cost and without any registration.

Why trust FinCalc Pro

Every calculator uses standard, widely accepted financial formulas. We continuously refine the tools for accuracy and ease of use. However, all results are estimates and should be used for informational purposes only. For major financial decisions, we always recommend consulting a qualified financial advisor.
